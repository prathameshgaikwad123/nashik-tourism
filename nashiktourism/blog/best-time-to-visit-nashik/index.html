Best Time to Visit Nashik
Month by Month Guide

Nashik offers something different every season — Kumbh Mela, SulaFest, monsoon beauty, harvest. Here is exactly when to visit based on what you want.

Travel TipsApril 20268 min read

Nashik is one of the few cities in India that offers genuinely worthwhile experiences across every season. The answer depends entirely on what you're looking for. This guide breaks it down by month and by travel type.

📅 Quick Answer

Kumbh Mela 2027: August–September 2027 • Wine & vineyards: November–February • Comfortable sightseeing: October–March • Monsoon beauty: July–September • Budget travel: April–June

Month by Month — Nashik Weather and What's On

January ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Weather: 10–25°C. Best weather of the year. What's on: Grape harvest begins at Sula. SulaFest (late January or early February). Ideal for combining pilgrimage with vineyard visits.

February ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Weather: 12–28°C. Warm, sunny, perfect. What's on: SulaFest (if not January). Full harvest season. Mahashivratri — one of the most spiritually electric nights of the year at Trimbakeshwar Jyotirlinga.

March ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Weather: 16–33°C. Getting warmer but still good mornings. What's on: Holi celebrated vibrantly. Harvest season winding down. New vintage wines releasing.

April ⭐⭐⭐

Weather: 22–38°C. Hot. Summer arriving. What's on: Gudi Padwa (Marathi New Year). Tourist crowds drop, hotels cheaper. Good for budget pilgrims.

May & June ⭐⭐

Weather: 25–42°C. Hot and dry. Pre-monsoon humidity in June. What's on: Quietest tourist period. Deepest hotel discounts. Best for those who prefer zero crowds at temples.

July & August ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Kumbh 2027)

Weather: 22–30°C. Monsoon. Heavy rain in hills, moderate in city. What's on in 2027: Amrit Snan August 2 and August 31, 2027. Despite monsoon, millions of pilgrims come. The rain during Amrit Snan is considered especially auspicious. Bhandardara and Igatpuri are stunning in monsoon for nature lovers.

September ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Kumbh 2027)

Weather: 22–31°C. Late monsoon easing. What's on in 2027: 3rd Amrit Snan September 11 (Nashik) and September 12 (Trimbakeshwar). Ganesh Chaturthi — massive celebrations across Maharashtra.

October ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Weather: 18–30°C. Post-monsoon. Everything green and beautiful. One of the best months. What's on: Navratri and Dussehra. Kumbh Mela continues with Parva Snan dates. Often overlooked but arguably Nashik's most beautiful month.

November ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Weather: 14–27°C. Ideal. Clear and cool. What's on: Vineyards lush before harvest. Diwali (October/November). Kartik Purnima — auspicious Kumbh Parva Snan date in 2027. Tourism season fully on.

December ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Weather: 10–22°C. Cool evenings. Perfect conditions but can feel cold at night. What's on: Peak season. Hotels busiest and most expensive. New vintage wines at Sula. Christmas and New Year events at vineyards.

Summary by Travel Type

  • First-time tourist: January or February — best weather, SulaFest, harvest season
  • Kumbh Mela pilgrim 2027: August 2, August 31, or September 11–12, 2027
  • Wine lover: November–March — harvest, tastings, SulaFest
  • Nature and trekking: October, or July–September for monsoon waterfalls at Bhandardara
  • Budget travel: April–June — heat but excellent prices
  • Festival experience: February (SulaFest), September (Ganesh Chaturthi), October (Navratri)

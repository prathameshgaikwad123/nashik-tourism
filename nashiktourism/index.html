Where to Go

Top Places to Visit
in Nashik

From sacred ghats to sunlit vineyards — Nashik offers one of India's most unique travel experiences.

Jyotirlinga · Spiritual

Trimbakeshwar Temple

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas and the sacred site of Kumbh Mela. The source of the holy Godavari river.
Heritage · Spiritual

Panchavati & Ramkund

The sacred fig grove where Lord Ram lived during his exile. Ramkund ghat is the primary Kumbh bathing site.
Wine · Leisure

Sula Vineyards

India's wine capital. Sula's sprawling estate produces award-winning wines amid the Sahyadri mountains.
History · Heritage

Pandavleni Caves

24 ancient Buddhist caves carved into the Trirashmi hill dating back to 3rd century BC.
Temple · Spiritual

Kalaram Temple

The famous black Ram idol temple in Panchavati — historically significant and deeply revered.
Nature · Weekend

Igatpuri & Bhandardara

Lush Sahyadri hill stations just an hour from Nashik — perfect for nature lovers and trekkers.
